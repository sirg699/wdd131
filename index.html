Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and is located in West Africa. It is known for its rich cultural diversity, with over 250 ethnic groups and languages. Nigeria has a vibrant economy and is a major producer of oil and natural gas. The country is also famous for its music, film industry (Nollywood), and diverse landscapes ranging from savannas to tropical rainforests.
